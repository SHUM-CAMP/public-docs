# SHUM

**SHUM** is a public non-governmental organization (NGO) founded by experts from the international [OSINT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open-source_intelligence) (Open Source Intelligence) community.

We use OSINT methods to investigate war crimes and protect human rights during the armed conflict initiated by Russia against Ukraine. We gather and analyze open-source data for investigative purposes and to support accountability.

---

## Mission

Since the beginning of the Russian–Ukrainian armed conflict, the international community has repeatedly documented war crimes. Many human rights violations in occupied territories and combat zones still go unnoticed and never reach judicial authorities because evidence is missing or incomplete.

We believe **the stories of war crimes victims must be heard and thoroughly investigated**.

---

## What we do

### OSINT investigations of war crimes

We collect and analyze open-source information to document Russia’s war crimes and preserve evidence for accountability.

### Consulting law enforcement and judicial authorities

We provide evidence, analysis, and expert input to help identify potential suspects and support lawful investigations and prosecutions.

### Countering disinformation and human rights work

We work to expose false narratives related to the conflict and support initiatives that protect human rights.

---

## Goals

- Collect and analyze evidence of violations of international humanitarian law using OSINT tools  
- Identify potential perpetrators where the facts support it  
- Support law enforcement and judicial authorities with consultations and expert analysis  

Our team includes OSINT researchers as well as lawyers, analysts, and experts in international law.
